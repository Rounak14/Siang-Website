 Projects: CU

Project details
The chair "CU!“ is designed taking inspiration from Asiatic crafts. The unusual fact that makes CU chair special is that it is made completely from one piece, which lends it an especially high degree of stability. By virtue of the weather-resistant material, minimal target surface for wind, and possibility for stacking, the chair is especially suited for terraces or caf√©s. The wire chair is available in white, silver, red, blue, and yellow.

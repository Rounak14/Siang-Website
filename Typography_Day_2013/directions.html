Reaching IIT Guwahati


Guwahati is well connected to the rest of India via airways and railways. For our international visitors, most of the international flights to and from India are handled by the international airports at New Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai and Chennai, which in turn, have a number of travel options to and from Guwahati.
